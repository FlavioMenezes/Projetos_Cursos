My name is Flávio. I get up at 5 in the morning. I do breakfast at 7 o'clock. I get to work at 9. I check emails. I sometimes go run at 8 p.m. I go to bed at midnight.

I was born in 1990. I got my first car when I was 18 years old. After I graduated from high school. After I got a good job. I met my wife at party. We fell in love and got married in 2014. After I got a promotion. We will our child when move to a bigger house.

My neighborhood is big. There's an Mexican restaurant across the street. There's a supermarket and store wine. There are a lot of good restaurants and pizza places. There's a great coffee shop.It's between the bookstore and supermarket. I go there everyday, but is too expensive. They have delicious cookies. I love coffee shop.

My apartment is very nice. It's a comfortable but is far from my work. The kitchen is modern and lovely, but it's small. The living room is great. I have a big TV. The bedroom is small, but it has a new bed and wardrobe. The bathroom is a very small. The neighborhood's very safe, but isn't near public transportation.

I love traveling. I traveled to several cities in Brazil and once for United States. I went for beaches in Bahia. It's the most beautiful place, and the night sky is gorgeous. I've visited "25 de Março" in São Paulo, but is very crowded. Almost impossible to walk. I've been to the Disney World in Miami, but I was very young, I had seven years old. But I remember almost everything. Was amazing, feather which is very expensive.I haven't been to New York City, but I really want to go. I would visit Central Park.
